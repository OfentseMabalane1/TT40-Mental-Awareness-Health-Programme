About TT40

TT40 was founded by Tsholofelo Tolo-Tlhale, driven by the mission to let people know they are not alone in their mental health journeys. The organization aims to educate people about mental health, reduce stigma, and provide a platform for open discussions.

Recent Event

TT40 hosted its first event on November 4th, where social workers spoke to the community about mental health, and an open platform was provided for people to share their stories. The event also featured an icebreaker and comedians to help create a relaxed atmosphere.
